We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days), express shipping (2-3 business days), and next day delivery (order before 2 PM). We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be unworn, unwashed, and with all tags attached. For exchanges, please return the item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item. Some items like intimate apparel, swimwear, and sale items are non-returnable. Please check our full return policy for more details.

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's website.
