Shipping Policy

1. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation.

2. Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping methods:

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 business days)
  • Express Shipping (2-3 business days)
  • Next Day Delivery (1 business day, order before 2 PM)

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight of your order and your location. You can view the shipping cost at checkout before completing your purchase.

4. Free Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on orders over $100 within the continental United States.

5. International Shipping

We currently ship to select countries. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

6. Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number.

7. Delivery Issues

If you experience any issues with your delivery, please contact our customer support team at fashionfusionez@outlook.com.
