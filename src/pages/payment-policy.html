Payment Policy

1. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay

2. Payment Security

All payments are processed securely through encrypted SSL technology. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers.

3. Payment Timing

Your payment will be processed immediately upon placing your order.

4. Currency

All prices are listed and charged in USD.

5. Taxes

Applicable taxes will be calculated and added to your total at checkout based on your shipping address.

6. Payment Issues

If you experience any issues with payment, please contact our customer support team at info@fashionfusion.com.
